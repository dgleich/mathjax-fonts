PT Sans — Accent Positioning

Single-letter accents (large)

\(\hat{a}\)   \(\hat{x}\)   \(\hat{A}\)   \(\hat{H}\)   \(\hat{M}\)
\(\tilde{a}\)   \(\tilde{x}\)   \(\tilde{A}\)   \(\tilde{H}\)   \(\tilde{M}\)
\(\bar{a}\)   \(\bar{x}\)   \(\bar{A}\)   \(\bar{H}\)   \(\bar{M}\)
\(\dot{a}\)   \(\dot{x}\)   \(\dot{A}\)   \(\dot{H}\)   \(\dot{M}\)
\(\ddot{a}\)   \(\ddot{x}\)   \(\ddot{A}\)   \(\ddot{H}\)   \(\ddot{M}\)
\(\vec{a}\)   \(\vec{x}\)   \(\vec{A}\)   \(\vec{H}\)   \(\vec{M}\)

Wide accents (large)

\(\widehat{a}\)   \(\widehat{ab}\)   \(\widehat{abc}\)   \(\widehat{abcde}\)
\(\widetilde{a}\)   \(\widetilde{ab}\)   \(\widetilde{abc}\)   \(\widetilde{abcde}\)
\(\overline{a}\)   \(\overline{ab}\)   \(\overline{abc}\)   \(\overline{abcde}\)

Accents on Greek

\(\hat{\alpha}\)   \(\tilde{\beta}\)   \(\bar{\gamma}\)   \(\dot{\theta}\)   \(\vec{\sigma}\)

In expressions

\(\hat{f}(x) = \sum_{k=0}^n \hat{a}_k x^k\)   \(\tilde{x} = x - \bar{x}\)   \(\dot{q} = \frac{\partial H}{\partial p}\)
\(\widehat{AB} = \widehat{A}\widehat{B}\)   \(\widetilde{f \circ g} = \widetilde{f} \circ \widetilde{g}\)   \(\overline{z_1 + z_2} = \overline{z_1} + \overline{z_2}\)
